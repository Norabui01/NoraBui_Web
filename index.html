Activities & Awards

I have served as the President of the Mathematical Association of America (MAA) for the past year and a half. In that role, I am leading our MAA chapter with the mission of fostering passion, talent, and promoting diversity in mathematics, science, and engineering. At the MAA conference at Ole Miss this past March, I achieved second place in the Integration Bee, only the third student in the history of McNees to do this.

Additionally, under my leadership, MAA is planning to host a local math competition at McNeese early next year to prepare teams to compete at the 2024 MAA conference in New Orleans. We are working diligently to include at least 100 students from all majors to participate. In May 2023, I was honored to be selected by the Math Department to join Pi Mu Epsilon. In addition, I am a member of the Association for Computing Machinery and the Robotics Club. During E-week, I built a small delivery robot and was involved in showing high school kids how it works with games.
